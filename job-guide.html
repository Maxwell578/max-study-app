💼 Job Application Guide

Steps to apply for your dream job:

  1. Prepare your CV: Highlight your skills and achievements.
  2. Write a cover letter: Make it personal and specific.
  3. Search regularly: Use job sites and networking.
  4. Practice interviews: Be confident and clear.
  5. Follow up: Send thank-you emails after interviews.
